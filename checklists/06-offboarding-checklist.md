## Emergency Offboarding (If Needed)

### Rapid Exit Checklist
```
When relationship ends abruptly:

 Document everything immediately
 Remove all access within 24 hours
 Export any work completed
 Send formal notice via email
 Invoice for work completed
 Rotate any shared credentials
 Preserve records for potential disputes
 Consult legal if needed
```
